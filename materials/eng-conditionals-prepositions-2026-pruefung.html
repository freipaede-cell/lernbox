Part A — Conditionals (24 Punkte)

1MC2 P.

Which conditional type is used for general truths and facts?

Lösung anzeigen
c) Zero Conditional — allgemeine Wahrheiten, Naturgesetze.
2Lückentext2 P.

Complete the Zero Conditional:

If you (mix) red and blue, you (get) purple.

Lösung anzeigen
If you mix red and blue, you get purple. (Present Simple + Present Simple)
3Lückentext2 P.

Complete the Conditional I:

If she (arrive) on time, we (not miss) the film.

Lösung anzeigen
If she arrives on time, we won't miss the film. (Present Simple + will + Infinitiv)
4Lückentext2 P.

Complete the Conditional II:

If I (be) you, I (apologise) immediately.

Lösung anzeigen
If I were you, I would apologise immediately. (Past Simple + would + Infinitiv. Beachte: were für alle Personen!)
5Lückentext2 P.

Complete the Conditional III:

If they (leave) earlier, they (catch) the train.

Lösung anzeigen
If they had left earlier, they would have caught the train. (Past Perfect + would have + PP)
6Lückentext2 P.

Complete the Conditional III:

If I (know) about the party, I (come).

Lösung anzeigen
If I had known about the party, I would have come. (Past Perfect + would have + PP)
7Zuordnung4 P.

Identify the conditional type (Zero, I, II, or III):

a) If I won the lottery, I would buy a house.
b) If you had told me, I would have helped you.
c) If it rains tomorrow, I'll stay home.
d) If you freeze water, it turns into ice.

Lösung anzeigen
a) Conditional II (hypothetisch, Gegenwart)
b) Conditional III (irreal, Vergangenheit)
c) Conditional I (wahrscheinlich, Zukunft)
d) Zero Conditional (Fakt/Naturgesetz)
(1 P. pro richtige Zuordnung)
8Fehlerkorrektur4 P.

Find and correct the error in each sentence:

a) If I would have studied harder, I would have passed.
b) If she will come tomorrow, we can go together.
c) If he was a bird, he would fly to Africa.
d) If they had took the bus, they wouldn't have been late.

Lösung anzeigen
a) If I had studied harder, I would have passed. (kein would im if-clause!)
b) If she comes tomorrow, we can go together. (kein will im if-clause!)
c) If he were a bird, he would fly to Africa. (were statt was)
d) If they had taken the bus, they wouldn't have been late. (Past Participle: taken)
(1 P. pro Korrektur)
9Satzbildung4 P.

Write complete Conditional III sentences using the prompts:

a) she / not eat so much / she / not feel sick
b) we / bring a map / we / not get lost

Lösung anzeigen
a) If she hadn't eaten so much, she wouldn't have felt sick. (2 P.)
b) If we had brought a map, we wouldn't have got/gotten lost. (2 P.)
(1 P. für korrekten if-clause, 1 P. für korrekten main clause)

Part B — Regrets: could have / should have (12 Punkte)

10MC2 P.

«I should have studied more.» — What does this express?

Lösung anzeigen
b) — should have drückt Bedauern aus: Ich hätte mehr lernen sollen (habe es aber nicht getan).
11Lückentext3 P.

Fill in should have or could have + the correct verb form:

a) You (tell) me earlier — I'm really annoyed! (Kritik)

b) We (take) the train, but we decided to drive. (Möglichkeit)

c) She (not say) that — it was really rude. (Kritik)

Lösung anzeigen
a) should have told (Kritik/Bedauern → should have)
b) could have taken (verpasste Möglichkeit → could have)
c) shouldn't have said (Kritik: es war falsch → shouldn't have)
12Übersetzung3 P.

Translate into English:

a) Ich hätte mehr schlafen sollen.
b) Du hättest mitkommen können.
c) Er hätte das nicht kaufen sollen.

Lösung anzeigen
a) I should have slept more.
b) You could have come along / come with us.
c) He shouldn't have bought that.
13Situativ4 P.

React to these situations using should have, shouldn't have, or could have:

a) You forgot your friend's birthday. →

b) You ate too much cake and now you feel sick. →

c) You missed the bus because you didn't run. →

d) Your friend failed the test because he played games all night. →

Lösung anzeigen
Mögliche Antworten:
a) I should have remembered / written it down.
b) I shouldn't have eaten so much cake.
c) I could have caught it if I had run.
d) He should have studied instead of playing games. / He shouldn't have played games all night.
(1 P. pro sinnvolle Antwort mit korrekter Struktur)

Part C — Prepositions (24 Punkte)

14Lückentext6 P.

Fill in the correct time preposition (in, on, at, since, for, during, by, until):

a) The test is Tuesday.

b) I was born March.

c) She has lived in Zurich 2018.

d) We have to finish 5 o'clock.

e) He fell asleep the lesson.

f) I've been waiting two hours.

Lösung anzeigen
a) on Tuesday (Tag)
b) in March (Monat)
c) since 2018 (Zeitpunkt)
d) by 5 o'clock (spätestens)
e) during the lesson (während)
f) for two hours (Zeitdauer)
15Lückentext3 P.

Fill in in, on, or at — or leave blank (Ø):

a) I'll see you next Monday.

b) The concert starts 8 pm Saturday.

c) We always go skiing winter.

d) She called me the evening.

e) What are you doing tonight?

Lösung anzeigen
a) Ø next Monday (next → keine Präposition!)
b) at 8 pm on Saturday
c) in winter
d) in the evening
e) Ø tonight (keine Präposition!)
(0.5 P. pro Lücke)
16Lückentext6 P.

Fill in the correct preposition after the verb:

a) Can you listen me, please?

b) I'm looking my phone. Have you seen it?

c) The result depends the weather.

d) He apologised being late.

e) I completely agree you.

f) She succeeded passing the exam.

Lösung anzeigen
a) to (listen to)
b) for (look for = suchen)
c) on (depend on)
d) for (apologise for)
e) with (agree with)
f) in (succeed in)
17Lückentext6 P.

Fill in the correct preposition after the adjective:

a) She's really good drawing.

b) I'm interested science fiction.

c) He's afraid heights.

d) We're very proud our team.

e) This book is different the movie.

f) She's worried her test results.

Lösung anzeigen
a) at (good at)
b) in (interested in)
c) of (afraid of)
d) of (proud of)
e) from (different from)
f) about (worried about)
18Gemischt3 P.

Fill in the correct preposition (noun combinations):

a) What's the reason the delay?

b) There's no solution this problem.

c) Smoking has a bad effect your health.

Lösung anzeigen
a) for (reason for)
b) to (solution to)
c) on (effect on)
